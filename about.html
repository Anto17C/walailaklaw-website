About Us

Who We Are

The Firm Behind
The Advice

Walailak Law Firm (สำนักงานกฎหมายวลัยลักษณ์ ทนายความ) is a Thai law firm providing structured legal representation for individuals, families, and businesses operating in Rayong · Pattaya · Chonburi · Bangkok and across Thailand.

We assist foreign residents, expatriates, and international investors who require clear communication and practical legal guidance under Thai law. Our objective is to bridge the gap between complex local regulations and the needs of global clients through professional, transparent legal support.

Our Approach

Legal matters in Thailand often require careful navigation of procedural rules, language requirements, and regulatory frameworks. We provide structured legal advice with clear explanations in English. We prioritise:

  • ›Careful preparation of legal documentation
  • ›Clear communication throughout proceedings
  • ›Professional representation before Thai courts and authorities
  • ›Compliance with Thai Civil, Commercial, and regulatory law

Our goal is not simply to handle cases, but to provide reliable legal guidance that protects long-term interests.

Practice Areas

Local Knowledge

Local Knowledge With

Ethical Standards

Commitment To Ethical Practice
