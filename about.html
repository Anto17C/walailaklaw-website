About Walailak Law Firm

Professional legal representation for foreigners and locals, built around clear communication and structured legal strategy.

Walailak Keawmai
Founder & Attorney

Walailak Law Firm (สำนักงานภูมหมายวาลัยลักษณ์ ทนายความ) is a Thai law firm providing structured legal representation for individuals, families, and businesses operating in Rayong, Pattaya, Chonburi and Bangkok, and across Thailand.

We assist foreign residents, expatriates, and international investors who require clear communication and practical legal guidance under Thai law. Our objective is to bridge the gap between complex local regulations and the needs of global clients through professional, transparent legal support.

Our approach

Legal matters in Thailand often require careful navigation of procedural rules, language requirements, and regulatory frameworks. Our firm provides structured legal advice with clear explanations in English, ensuring that clients understand their rights, risks, and available options. We prioritise:

  • Careful preparation of legal documentation
  • Clear communication throughout proceedings
  • Professional representation before Thai courts and authorities
  • Compliance with Thai Civil, Commercial, and regulatory law

A network when you need it

Every matter is led personally by Walailak. For specialized or larger cases, she brings in one of 7+ trusted partner attorneys — so clients get direct, personal attention with the depth of a full legal team behind it.

What we handle

Experience in key practice areas

  • Criminal litigation and bail applications
  • Civil disputes and contract enforcement
  • Family law matters
  • Company registration and corporate structuring
  • Real estate transactions and due diligence
  • Arbitration and dispute resolution
  • Legal document drafting and notarization

Local knowledge, national reach

Our primary service area is Rayong and Pattaya, a key industrial and commercial hub within Thailand's Eastern Economic Corridor. We also represent clients in Chonburi, Bangkok and across Thailand depending on the jurisdiction and legal matter.

Commitment to ethical practice

We conduct all legal representation in accordance with professional ethical standards and regulatory requirements. Integrity, confidentiality, and procedural accuracy guide every matter we undertake.
