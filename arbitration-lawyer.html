Arbitration lawyer services for commercial disputes

Arbitration is a recognised method of alternative dispute resolution used to resolve commercial disputes outside the traditional court system. Our experienced arbitration lawyer represents domestic and international clients in proceedings in Rayong, Pattaya, Chonburi, and Bangkok.

Our expert arbitration lawyer provides legal representation in contract disputes, shareholder conflicts, construction claims, and cross-border commercial matters under the Thai Arbitration Act.

Arbitration advantages

  • Confidentiality — proceedings are private, protecting sensitive commercial information
  • Procedural flexibility — parties may agree on venue, language, and governing law
  • Specialised expertise — arbitrators can be selected based on industry experience
  • International enforcement — Thailand is a signatory to the New York Convention

Our arbitration lawyer services

We represent clients before major arbitration institutions in Thailand, including the Thai Arbitration Institute (TAI) and the Thailand Arbitration Center (THAC). Our services include:

  • Drafting and reviewing arbitration clauses
  • Representation in arbitration proceedings
  • Preparation of submissions and evidence
  • Enforcement of arbitral awards before Thai courts

Industry sectors we assist

  • Construction and infrastructure projects
  • Shareholder and joint venture disputes
  • International trade and manufacturing
  • Commercial contract breaches
