Reliable family law services for foreigners

Family law matters require careful legal guidance and a balanced approach, particularly when they involve cross-border issues or international families. Our expert family lawyer assists clients with divorce, child custody, parental authority, asset division, and prenuptial agreements under Thai law.

Whether your matter requires mediation or court representation, our firm provides structured legal advice to protect your rights and your family's future.

Mediation first, where appropriate

Where appropriate, we prioritise mediation to resolve disputes efficiently and reduce emotional and financial strain — offering cost-effective resolution, greater privacy, and legally enforceable agreements. When court proceedings are necessary, our lawyers provide representation before the relevant Family Court.
