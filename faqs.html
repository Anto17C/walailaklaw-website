Criminal Law & Bail

Bail in Thailand allows a defendant to be temporarily released while awaiting trial. The court determines the amount of security required based on the seriousness of the offence and flight risk. Bail may be granted in the form of cash, assets, or a guarantor. Read more.
Yes. Foreign nationals have the same fundamental right to apply for bail as Thai citizens, though courts often require higher security amounts and may impose travel restrictions.
Authorities may detain a suspect for up to 48 hours for investigation before requiring a court order for further detention. You are entitled to legal counsel during this window.

Civil & Family Law

The duration depends on complexity, court schedules, and whether mediation resolves the dispute early. Some cases conclude within months, while more complex matters may take longer. Read more.
Marital property acquired during the marriage is generally divided equally under Thai law, subject to any prenuptial agreement in place.
Custody decisions are based on the best interests of the child, considering each parent's circumstances and ability to provide care.
Uncontested administrative divorces can be completed quickly. Contested divorces requiring court proceedings take longer, depending on complexity.

Business & Investment Law

Foreign ownership may be restricted under the Foreign Business Act. However, certain exceptions apply, including Board of Investment (BOI) promotion or specific treaty privileges. Proper legal structuring is essential. Read more.
A common benchmark is 2 million THB in registered capital per foreign work permit, alongside required Thai employee ratios.
Generally, foreigners cannot own land freehold, but can own condominium units within the foreign quota, or use long-term leasehold and usufruct structures.

Legal Documents & Notarization

Many documents intended for use overseas require notarization by a Notarial Services Attorney in Thailand before being recognised by foreign authorities.
A valid Thai Will must meet specific execution formalities under Thai law. We assist with drafting Thai-compliant wills to ensure proper distribution of assets and simplify probate.
