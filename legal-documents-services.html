Legal document drafting & notarial services

Accurate legal documentation is essential to protect your personal, business, and property interests in Thailand. Our firm provides professional drafting, review, certification, and notarization services for foreign and international clients.

Core services we handle

  • Drafting and review of commercial and personal contracts
  • Power of Attorney preparation for Land Office, company, and banking matters
  • Last Wills and estate documentation
  • Affidavits and sworn statements
  • Document certification and notarization

Power of Attorney (POA)

A Power of Attorney authorises another person to act on your behalf for specified legal matters — particularly important for foreign nationals who may not be physically present to complete transactions. We prepare General and Special Powers of Attorney tailored to property transfers, company filings, and banking matters.

Last Will & notarial certification

Last Will and Testament

For individuals holding assets in Thailand, a properly drafted Thai Will ensures clear distribution of property and avoids statutory intestacy rules. We assist with drafting Thai-compliant wills, advising on asset distribution, and ensuring proper execution formalities. A local Will can significantly simplify probate proceedings for beneficiaries.

Notarial & document certification

In Thailand, notarization is performed by a Notarial Services Attorney registered with the Lawyers Council of Thailand. We provide signature witnessing, certification of copies, and affidavit notarization for foreign use or embassy submission. Clients must attend in person with valid identification for notarization services.
