1. Power Of Attorney (POA) In Thailand

A Power of Attorney authorises another person to act on your behalf for specified legal matters. This is particularly important for foreign nationals who may not be physically present in Thailand to complete transactions.

We prepare General and Special Powers of Attorney tailored to:

  • Property transfers and Land Office transactions
  • Company registration and corporate filings
  • Banking and financial matters

Where required, documents are prepared in the format accepted by Thai government agencies.

2. Last Will And Testament

For individuals holding assets in Thailand, a properly drafted Thai Will ensures clear distribution of property and avoids statutory intestacy rules. We assist with:

  • Drafting Thai-compliant Wills
  • Advising on asset distribution under Thai law
  • Ensuring proper execution formalities

A local Will can significantly simplify probate proceedings for beneficiaries.

3. Notarial And Document Certification Services

In Thailand, notarization is performed by a Notarial Services Attorney registered with the Lawyers Council of Thailand. Our experienced legal documents lawyers provide:

  • Signature witnessing and identity verification
  • Certification of copies of documents
  • Affidavit notarization for foreign use
  • Documentation required by embassies and international authorities

Clients must attend in person with valid identification for notarization services.
